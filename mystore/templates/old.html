I'm Darnell Ferguson, also known as "Super Chef". I am 23 years old, a father, Executive chef and Founder of Cookingin10.com. I was recruited out of high school by one of the top culinary schools in the nation, Sullivan University where I graduated as one of the top Chefs in my class. Most people are surprised at how much I have accomplished in my profession at such a young age. At 21 years of age I was honored to travel to China as an Olympic Chef in the 2008 Summer Olympics. In my young career I have done it all, from cooking for athletes, government Officials, and even President of the United State George H. Bush.

What makes me different from other Chefs? The First thing that makes me unique is my appearance and the way I make cooking easy and comfortable. I often wear baseball caps matching my chef coat, because for me, cooking is about being relaxed, being yourself and having fun. My friends say I think like an old man sometimes because I'm always thinking 2 steps ahead. My dream is to open the door for young chefs to get the recognition that the older chefs on TV get. I created Cookingin10.com with two main goals: to help people understand how to make the time in the kitchen fun and to give people an alternative to other cooking sites out there.
