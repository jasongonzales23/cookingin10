About Me

I'm Darnell Ferguson, also known as "Super Chef". I am Executive chef and Founder of Cookingin10.com. I was recruited out of high school by one of the top culinary schools in the nation, Sullivan University where I graduated as one of the top Chefs in my class. Most people are surprised at how much I have accomplished in my profession at such a young age. At 21 years of age I was honored to travel to China as an Olympic Chef in the 2008 Summer Olympics. In my young career I have done it all, from cooking for athletes, government Officials, and even President George H. Bush.

I created Cookingin10.com with two main goals: to help people understand how to make the time in the kitchen fun and to give people an alternative to other cooking sites out there.
